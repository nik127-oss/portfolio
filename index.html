01 / ABOUT

An engineer who reads datasheets for fun.

Abstracts don't interest me. The translation from instruction to transistor does.

My work sits at the seam between architecture and silicon — the decisions that make a processor fast, efficient, and verifiable. I spend time in Cadence Virtuoso, Genus, Innovus, Xcelium and on FPGA toolchains, because the most interesting engineering problems don't show up until the gates meet the layout.

I learn by building. I'd rather tape out a small block correctly than read three papers about a big one. That's why my projects are full flows — schematic to GDSII, RTL to bitstream, theory to silicon-signoff.

Long-term, I'm aiming at CPU / SoC architecture — pipeline design, hazard handling, memory hierarchy, and hardware-software co-design for emerging workloads like ML inference and quantum control.

P.01 · FPGA / Edge AI

A 1D CNN accelerator on Zynq-7020 for 77 GHz radar target classification

// Vivado HLS · PYNQ-Z2 · AXI-Lite · INT8 Quantization · PS–PL Co-design · April 2026

Deployed a hardware-accelerated 1D CNN on the Xilinx Zynq-7020 FPGA for real-time classification of drones, birds, humans, and corner reflectors using 77 GHz FMCW radar micro-Doppler data across 75,868 signal segments. Achieving 141× speedup over ARM-only inference at 1.3 ms end-to-end latency — measured on the actual PYNQ-Z2 board, not estimated.

P.02 · Analog / Mixed-Signal

A clocked StrongARM latch comparator in gpdk180nm CMOS — full custom analog flow

// Virtuoso · Spectre · Quantus QRC · gpdk180nm · Monte Carlo · April 2026

Designed a clocked regenerative StrongARM latch comparator in Cadence Virtuoso using gpdk180nm technology with a custom symbol for the clocked differential amplifier topology. Achieving 148 ps propagation delay at 17.55 µW dynamic power with zero static power consumption and 3.79 GHz bandwidth.

  • Robustness under PVT sweeps (−40°C to 125°C, ±10% VDD), five process corners (NN/FF/SS/FS/SF), and Monte Carlo across 13 parameters

P.03 · Full ASIC Flow

A full RTL-to-GDSII UART transceiver — from Verilog to signoff-clean layout

// Verilog · Xcelium · Genus · Innovus · DRC/LVS · 9-Metal Signoff · April 2026

Designed a parameterizable 8-N-1 UART transceiver in Verilog with hierarchical TX/RX/top modules and dual independent 5-state FSMs. Pushed through the entire ASIC flow to DRC/LVS-clean GDSII — placed, routed, and physically verified with 9 metal layers and +38.1 ns timing slack at 25 MHz.

P.04 · Accepted at SPARC 2026

An ancilla-free quantum circuit for Hamming weight — accepted at SPARC 2026

// Qiskit · Bloch Sphere Analysis · Approximate QFT · NISQ-aware Optimization

Designed an ancilla-free quantum circuit for Hamming weight computation achieving up to 69% T-gate reduction under NISQ constraints. Optimized via controlled phase rotation truncation and approximate QFT, trading gate depth against hardware fidelity. Validated in Qiskit using statevector simulation and Bloch sphere analysis. Paper accepted at SPARC Conference 2026.
